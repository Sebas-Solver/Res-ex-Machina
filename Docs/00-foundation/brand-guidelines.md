# Brand Guidelines — Res ex Machina

This document defines the core visual identity elements for **Res ex Machina**, establishing consistency across all platforms, documentation, and graphical interfaces.

## 1. The Logo: "El Lazo" (The Loop)

The primary symbol of Res ex Machina is a continuous, Mobius-like ribbon (the "Lazo").

**Symbolism & Concept:**
- **Continuous Record:** The unbroken loop represents an immutable chain of custody and the continuity of verified data.
- **Machine Intelligence:** The fluid geometry and ribbon structure imply adaptive systems and intelligent verification.
- **Registry & Trust:** The loop conveys a closed, self-validating system—built for trust and transparency.
- **Permanent by Design:** A timeless form that communicates stability, resilience, and enduring provenance.

*Logo files are located in `docs/assets/Logo lazo - RxM.png`.*

## 2. Color Palette

The color system is designed to feel highly technical, reliable, and premium.

| Color Name     | HEX       | Usage |
|----------------|-----------|-------|
| **Electric Blue** | `#0066FF` | Primary accent, calls to action, active states, and logo core color. |
| **Charcoal**      | `#0F1115` | Primary background, deep dark mode surface. |
| **Slate Gray**    | `#6B7280` | Secondary text, borders, inactive elements, subtle contrasts. |

## 3. Typography

**Sora** is the official typeface for Res ex Machina.
It is an early-geometric sans-serif font, selected for its crispness, legibility, and technical yet approachable character.

- **Headings:** Sora (SemiBold / Bold)
- **Body Text:** Sora (Light / Regular)
- **Status/Data:** Sora (Medium / SemiBold, with tabular-nums enabled for metrics)

## 4. Voice and Tone

Res ex Machina is an infrastructure protocol. The voice should be:
- **Neutral & Objective:** We do not judge the content, we only record the facts of its generation.
- **Technical & Authoritative:** Precise terminology, favoring clarity over marketing fluff.
- **Transparent:** Always clear about what the system can and cannot do.
